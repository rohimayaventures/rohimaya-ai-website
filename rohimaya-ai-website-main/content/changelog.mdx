# Development Timeline

Track our progress as we build AI-powered tools for healthcare teams.

---

## 2025

### November 2025
- **Website Launch**: Launched rohimaya.ai marketing site with product information
- **Pilot Program**: Opened applications for early design partners
- **Brand Finalization**: Completed Phoenix & Peacock visual identity system
- **Product Development**: Continued building EclipseLink AI and Phoenix & Peacock Honors prototypes

### Earlier 2025
- **Company Formation**: Established Rohimaya Ventures
- **Product Research**: Completed user research with 50+ nurses and clinicians
- **Technical Architecture**: Designed HIPAA-compliant infrastructure and data models
- **Brand Development**: Created "Where the Phoenix Rises and the Peacock Dances" brand narrative

---

## Roadmap

### Q1 2026 (Target)
- **EclipseLink Alpha Release**: First working prototype with voice capture and SBAR generation
- **Honors Alpha Release**: Initial gamification framework with point system
- **Pilot Launch**: Begin testing with 2-3 healthcare organizations
- **HIPAA Documentation**: Complete Business Associate Agreement templates

### Q2 2026 (Target)
- **EclipseLink Beta**: Add predictive alerting and role-based dashboards
- **Honors Beta**: Expand to full tier system (Bronze/Silver/Gold/Platinum)
- **Pilot Expansion**: Scale to 5+ healthcare sites based on feedback
- **Integration Work**: Begin EHR integration design (Epic, Cerner)

### Q3 2026 (Target)
- **Product Refinement**: Bug fixes and UX improvements from pilot feedback
- **Security Certifications**: Begin SOC 2 Type II audit process
- **Commercial Pricing**: Finalize subscription tiers and enterprise bundles
- **Sales Enablement**: Create demo videos and training materials

### Q4 2026 (Target)
- **Commercial Launch**: General availability of EclipseLink AI v1.0
- **First Paying Customers**: Target 3-5 healthcare organizations
- **Honors GA**: General availability of Phoenix & Peacock Honors v1.0
- **Future Products**: Begin design work on Rounds Assist, Intake Navigator, SignalWatch

---

## Future Vision (2027+)

Long-term product roadmap includes:

**Rounds Assist** (2027 target)
- Voice-guided bedside rounds tool
- Fall risk assessment and intervention tracking
- Shift checklist automation

**Intake Navigator** (2027 target)
- AI-powered patient intake assistance
- Admission checklist and risk flagging
- Smoother transitions of care

**SignalWatch** (2027 target)
- Real-time risk monitoring across units
- Multi-patient alerting dashboard
- Continuous patient deterioration tracking

**Platform Features** (2027+ target)
- Public API for third-party integrations
- Mobile apps (iOS/Android) with offline mode
- Multi-language support (Spanish, Mandarin, Tagalog)
- Enterprise analytics dashboard

**Note:** All dates are targets and subject to change based on pilot feedback and development progress.
