# Phoenix & Peacock Honors™

## Overview

Phoenix & Peacock Honors™ is a compliance-friendly gamification system designed to recognize and reward clinical excellence, safety achievements, and collaborative teamwork. By celebrating contributions transparently, it boosts morale, reduces turnover, and aligns staff goals with organizational quality targets.

## Honor Tiers

### Bronze
**Entry-Level Recognition**
- Consistent shift attendance
- Zero safety incidents (30-day periods)
- Completion of mandatory training
- Peer acknowledgments

### Silver
**Intermediate Honors**
- Process improvement suggestions implemented
- Mentorship of new staff members
- High patient satisfaction scores (>90%)
- Advanced certifications earned

### Gold
**Advanced Recognition**
- Innovation in workflow or patient care
- Quality leadership roles (e.g., unit councils)
- Patient advocacy initiatives
- Sustained excellence (6+ months)

### Platinum
**Elite Honors**
- Transformational impact on care quality
- System-wide process improvements
- Published research or presentations
- Exceptional peer and patient recognition

## Earning Mechanisms

### Safety Milestones
- Zero falls, medication errors, or HAIs over specified periods
- Participation in safety huddles and root cause analyses

### Efficiency & Quality
- Completing handoffs consistently ahead of schedule
- Maintaining high documentation accuracy
- Meeting or exceeding quality benchmarks

### Collaboration
- Peer nominations for teamwork and support
- Cross-departmental project participation
- Mentorship hours logged

### Patient Engagement
- Positive patient feedback and comments
- High satisfaction scores on HCAHPS surveys
- Patient education initiative participation

### Continuous Learning
- Training module completion
- Attendance at conferences or workshops
- Skills certifications and competencies

## Leaderboards & Recognition

### Unit-Level Boards
- Foster healthy competition among teams
- Celebrate collective achievements
- Privacy-protected opt-in display

### Individual Achievements
- Personal dashboards with progress tracking
- Badge collections and milestone markers
- Exportable achievement summaries for resumes

### Monthly Spotlights
- Featured stories of honor recipients
- Newsletter highlights and social media recognition
- Leadership acknowledgment events

## Rewards Integration

- Professional development stipends
- Conference attendance opportunities
- Gift programs (where permitted)
- Preferred scheduling options (where applicable)
- Recognition in organizational communications

## Compliance & Fairness

### Union-Friendly Design
- No forced ranking or punitive metrics
- Transparent, objective earning criteria
- Equal opportunity across all roles and experience levels

### Privacy Controls
- Opt-in for public sharing of achievements
- Anonymized leaderboards available
- Individual control over visibility settings

### Equity & Inclusion
- Accessible to all staff, regardless of shift or department
- Multiple pathways to earn honors
- Regular audits to ensure fair distribution

## Impact Metrics

- **Retention**: Reduced turnover by recognizing contributions
- **Morale**: Celebrate wins in high-pressure environments
- **Alignment**: Tie honors to quality and safety goals
- **Insights**: Track engagement trends and improvement areas
