# Rounds Assist

**Voice-Guided Rounds + Task Capture**

Rounds Assist streamlines physician and nurse rounding workflows with AI-powered voice capture, automated task lists, and integrated patient summaries.

---

## Overview

Clinical rounds are essential but time-consuming. Rounds Assist combines voice recognition, clinical context, and task automation to help providers focus on patients instead of paperwork.

---

## Key Features

### Voice-Guided Workflow
Speak naturally during rounds—AI captures orders, observations, and follow-up tasks.

### Automatic Task Lists
System generates to-do items for the care team based on round discussions.

### Patient Summary Integration
Pre-round briefings with overnight events, lab results, and flagged concerns.

### Multidisciplinary Coordination
Share round notes instantly with nursing, pharmacy, and case management.

---

## Healthcare Impact

- **Save 20+ min per round**: Less time documenting, more time at bedside
- **Reduce missed tasks**: Automated follow-up ensures nothing falls through cracks
- **Improve team communication**: Real-time updates visible to entire care team
