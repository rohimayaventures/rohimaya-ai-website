# EclipseLink AI

## Overview

EclipseLink AI is a voice-first clinical handoff platform that generates complete SBAR reports in under 30 seconds. Using natural language processing and machine learning, it automates documentation, maintains context across shifts, and provides early warning alerts for patient deterioration.

## Core Features

### 1. Voice Capture & Transcription
- Real-time voice-to-text conversion
- Medical terminology recognition
- Multilingual support (English, Spanish, Mandarin, Tagalog, and more)

### 2. Automated SBAR Generation
- **Situation**: Current patient status and chief complaint
- **Background**: Medical history, medications, allergies
- **Assessment**: Clinical findings and vital trends
- **Recommendation**: Suggested interventions and follow-up

### 3. Update-Only Documentation
- AI maintains patient context from previous shifts
- Clinicians only document what changed
- Automatic comparison highlighting

### 4. Predictive Analytics
- 6–12 hour early warning for deterioration risk
- Pattern recognition across vital signs, labs, and assessments
- Customizable alert thresholds by unit and acuity

### 5. Role-Based Dashboards
- **Nurses**: Quick handoff input and review
- **Physicians**: Clinical summaries and alerts
- **Administrators**: Quality metrics and compliance tracking

## Integration

- Epic, Cerner, Meditech EHRs
- Secure messaging (Slack, Teams, HIPAA-compliant platforms)
- Mobile apps (iOS/Android)
- Shift scheduling systems

## Security & Compliance

- HIPAA-compliant encryption (AES-256)
- Automated audit trails
- Role-based access controls
- SOC 2 Type II certification (planned for 2027)
