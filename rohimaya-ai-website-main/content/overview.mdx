# Rohimaya Health AI

**Clinical Handoffs, Reimagined**

Rohimaya Health AI combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with human-centered recognition systems to transform healthcare communication and staff engagement.

## Our Products

### EclipseLink AI
Voice-powered SBAR generation, predictive patient analytics, and seamless EHR integration. Reduce documentation time from 15 minutes to 30 seconds per patient while improving accuracy and safety.

### Phoenix & Peacock Honors™
A gamified recognition system that celebrates clinical excellence, safety milestones, and team collaboration. Drive engagement and retention through transparent, union-friendly honor tiers.

## Why Rohimaya?

- **Proven Time Savings**: 30-second handoffs vs. 15+ minute manual documentation
- **Early Warning System**: 6–12 hour predictive alerts for patient deterioration
- **Staff-First Design**: Built by healthcare professionals for healthcare professionals
- **Compliance Ready**: HIPAA-compliant, audit-friendly, union-compatible

## Mission

To eliminate preventable errors and burnout in healthcare through intelligent automation and meaningful recognition.
