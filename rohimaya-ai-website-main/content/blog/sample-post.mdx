# Why Clinical Handoffs Are Healthcare's Most Critical Workflow

Every day, millions of clinical handoffs occur across hospitals, clinics, and care facilities. These transitions of care—when one provider transfers patient responsibility to another—are both essential and dangerous.

---

## The Problem

Studies show that **80% of serious medical errors** involve miscommunication during handoffs. When shift changes happen, critical information can be lost:

- Medication changes not communicated
- Pending test results overlooked
- Patient allergies missed
- Care plan adjustments forgotten

Traditional handoff methods rely on:
- Handwritten notes (illegible, incomplete)
- Verbal reports (forgotten, misheard)
- EHR copy-paste (outdated, irrelevant)

---

## Why SBAR Alone Isn't Enough

The SBAR (Situation-Background-Assessment-Recommendation) framework is the gold standard for structured communication. But even with training, nurses spend **10-15 minutes per patient** on manual documentation.

In a 12-hour shift with 6 patients, that's **90+ minutes** just on handoff paperwork—time taken away from direct patient care.

---

## The AI Solution

EclipseLink AI transforms this workflow:

1. **Voice Capture**: Nurses speak naturally about patient status
2. **Auto-Structure**: AI formats into SBAR in real-time
3. **Context Awareness**: System pulls relevant data from EHR (labs, vitals, meds)
